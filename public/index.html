About Me

Software architect, developer, and automation engineer with experience in TypeScript, JavaScript/NodeJS, C#, web technologies, and more.

Hi, I'm Steven. I've been programming since I was in the 7th grade and started interning in my high school's IT department at 16 years old. Since then, I've worked on everything from NASA mainframes to VoIP start-ups, enterprise-scale shipping management, and commercial building security systems. I started in desktop support and server administration, and then spent a lot of my career in a development role. I've also worked in DevOps and QA, where I focused on designing tools and creating infrastructure to support test automation using Cucumber. When I'm not writing code or talking about software, you'll find me snow skiing, playing piano or guitar, or spending time with my wife and kids.
